Put on your headphones and position them correctly. 🎧

🔧 Volume Calibration

We'll play a 1000 Hz tone. 🎵

Adjust your computer's volume to a comfortable level before playing. This is the loudest sound you'll hear.



📝 Test Instructions

Here's how it works: 🔍

  • Hear beeps in left/right ears 👂
  • Click YES if you hear a beep, NO if not
  • Some trials are silent (normal) ❓
  • If you click NO, you'll get up to 2 retries at the same level
  • Test adjusts to find your threshold 📉
